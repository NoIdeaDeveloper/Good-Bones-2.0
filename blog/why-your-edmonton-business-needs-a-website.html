Why Your Edmonton Business Needs a Website That Actually Works

July 13, 2026 by Good Bones Team Web Design Edmonton Small Business

A pretty homepage isn't enough. Here's what makes a website a real asset for local Edmonton businesses.

Your website is often the first impression potential customers get of your business. For Edmonton businesses competing in a local market, that impression has to do more than look nice—it has to work.

What does "working" mean?

A website that works is fast, accessible, and clearly guides visitors toward action. It loads in under three seconds, reads well on phones, and makes it obvious how to book, buy, or get in touch.

  • Speed: Slow sites lose visitors before they read a word.
  • Clarity: Confusing navigation sends people back to Google.
  • Trust: Outdated design makes buyers hesitate.

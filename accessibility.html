Accessibility Statement

We believe the web should work for everyone. Here's how we approach accessible design and what we're doing to improve.

Last updated: July 12, 2026

Our commitment

Good Bones Web Inc. is committed to making our website and the websites we build usable by as many people as possible. We aim to follow recognized accessibility best practices, including the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

What we do to support accessibility

  • Use clear, readable typography and strong color contrast for text.
  • Structure pages with semantic HTML and proper heading order.
  • Add descriptive labels and alternative text for images and icons.
  • Ensure the site can be navigated with a keyboard.
  • Respect reduced-motion preferences for visitors who disable animations.
  • Test with keyboard navigation, screen readers, and common browser tools.

Accessibility in our client work

Accessibility is part of how we build websites for clients. We design with readable contrast, semantic markup, keyboard-friendly navigation, and responsive layouts. We also offer accessibility reviews and remediation for existing sites.

Known limitations

Some decorative animations and visual effects are present for brand personality. These do not affect core functionality, and visitors who prefer reduced motion can disable them through their system settings.

We are continuously learning and improving. If you find a barrier we have not addressed, please let us know.

Third-party content

Our site may include links to or embeds from third-party services. We choose accessible tools when possible, but we cannot guarantee the accessibility of every external platform.

How to give feedback

If you experience any difficulty using this website, or if you have suggestions for improving accessibility, we want to hear from you.

What this policy covers

This Privacy Policy explains what information Good Bones Web Inc. collects, how we use it, and the choices you have when you visit our website or use our services. We believe privacy should be straightforward, so we've kept this page short and readable.

Information we collect

We only collect information that helps us build great websites and communicate with you. This may include:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number, and business information you provide through our contact form or email.
  • Project details — information about your business, goals, timeline, and website needs.
  • Usage data — anonymous information about how visitors use our site, such as pages viewed and time spent, collected through standard analytics tools.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial details, health information, or government IDs through this website.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your messages and project inquiries.
  • Plan, build, and maintain your website.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Send occasional updates about our work, if you've asked to hear from us.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

How we store and protect information

We keep your information only as long as needed to fulfill the purpose it was collected for. We use reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Because we are a small consulting company, your information is shared only with the people directly involved in your project. We use trusted third-party tools for email, hosting, and analytics, and we choose tools that take privacy seriously.

Cookies and analytics

Our website may use small text files called cookies to help the site work properly and to understand how visitors use our pages. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

We may use simple analytics tools that collect anonymous data, such as which pages are visited most often. This data does not identify you personally.

External links

Our website may link to other websites, such as client projects or resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites, so we encourage you to read their policies when you visit them.

Your choices

You can ask us to:

  • Update or correct your contact information.
  • Delete your information from our records.
  • Stop sending you messages or marketing emails.

To make any of these requests, contact us at hello@goodbonesweb.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will change the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.

Contact us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please reach out.

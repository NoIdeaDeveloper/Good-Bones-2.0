Our promise

At Good Bones Web Inc., we treat your information with care. We only collect what we need, we keep it only as long as necessary, and we never sell it. This policy describes what we collect on this website and how we use it.

What we collect

We collect information in three situations:

  • When you contact us. If you fill out a form or send an email, we receive your name, email address, phone number if you provide one, and anything you tell us about your project or question.
  • When we work together. During a project, we may collect details about your business, timeline, goals, and the materials you want on your site.
  • When you browse our site. We collect anonymous, privacy-focused analytics such as which pages are visited and how long visitors stay. This data is not personally identifiable.

We do not collect sensitive information like financial details, health information, or government IDs through this website unless a specific project clearly requires it and you give explicit consent.

Why we collect it

We use your information only for clear, specific purposes:

  • To reply to you and discuss your project.
  • To plan and deliver the work you hired us for.
  • To improve our website and make it more useful.

If we ever need to use your information for something new, we will ask first or clearly explain why another legal reason applies.

How we protect it

We use physical, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect your information from loss, theft, and unauthorized access. We choose service providers carefully and require them to meet strong privacy and security standards.

If a breach ever creates a real risk of harm, we will notify affected users and report it to the appropriate authorities as quickly as possible.

Who else sees your information

We do not sell your information. We only share it with trusted service providers who help us run our business, such as web hosting, email, analytics, and payment processing. Each provider is contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

Some of these providers may process data outside Canada. When that happens, we assess the risks and put safeguards in place, such as contractual protections or approved certification programs.

How long we keep it

We keep personal information only as long as we need it to provide our services, respond to requests, meet legal obligations, or satisfy contract terms. When we no longer need it, we delete or securely anonymize it.

You can ask us to delete your information at any time, and we will comply unless a legal requirement prevents us from doing so.

Your rights

You have control over your personal information. You can:

  • Ask what we have. Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to fix it. Correct inaccurate or out-of-date information.
  • Ask us to delete it. Request that we delete your information, subject to legal limits.
  • Withdraw consent. Change your mind about how we use your information.
  • Make a complaint. Raise a concern about how we handle privacy.

We will respond to requests within 30 days, in plain language. If you need help preparing a request, we are happy to assist.

Cookies and analytics

Our site uses privacy-focused analytics that do not rely on invasive tracking cookies or cross-site advertising. We do not use advertising cookies or track you across other websites.

Children's privacy

Our services are for adults and businesses. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we learn we have collected a child's personal information, we will delete it promptly.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our services or the law change. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review the policy from time to time.

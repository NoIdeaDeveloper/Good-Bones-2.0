What we do

We design and build websites, provide strategy guidance, and help rescue struggling sites. Each project is scoped individually. Before work begins, we agree in writing on deliverables, timeline, and cost.

Quotes and payment

  • Quotes are valid for 14 days unless otherwise stated.
  • A deposit is usually required to begin work. The amount is specified in your project agreement.
  • Remaining payments are due according to the milestones or schedule in the agreement.
  • Late payments may delay project delivery.

Client responsibilities

To keep a project on track, clients agree to:

  • Provide content, branding assets, and feedback on time.
  • Review deliverables and approve or request revisions within the agreed timeframe.
  • Make sure they have the rights to anything they provide, such as photos, text, and logos.
  • Maintain their own accounts for third-party services unless we agree otherwise.

Revisions and scope changes

Each project includes a set number of revision rounds, defined in the agreement. Additional revisions or new requests outside the original scope may take extra time and cost extra. We will always discuss any scope change with you before proceeding.

After final payment, clients receive the right to use the final website and design deliverables for their business. We retain the right to show the work in our portfolio and marketing unless we agree otherwise in writing.

We do not claim ownership of any content, logos, trademarks, or other materials you already owned before the project.

Cancellations and refunds

If a project is cancelled after work has begun, the deposit is non-refundable. Any completed work beyond the deposit value will be billed proportionally. We will clearly communicate what has been completed at the time of cancellation.
